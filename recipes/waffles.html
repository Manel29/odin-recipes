Waffles

Description

A waffle is a cooked food made from a batter that consists of flour, water, baking powder, oil, and eggs.

It has a gridlike appearance because it is cooked in between two hot metal plates in an electric kitchen appliance called a waffle iron.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • â…“ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
